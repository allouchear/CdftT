11  hardness = Chemical hardness = (mu+ - mu-)

13  w = Electrophilicity index = mu^2/(2 hardness)
14  w- = propensity to donate electron = mu-^2/(2 hardness)
15  w+ = propensity to accept electron = mu+^2/(2 hardness)
16  S = Global softness = 1/hardness
17  Qmax = Maximal electronic charge accepted by an electrophile = -mu/hardness
18  DEmin = Energy decrease if the electrophile take Qmax = -mu^2/(2 hardness)
19  fk- = Local Fukui electrophilic attack

21  sk- = Local softness electrophilic attack = S fk-
22  sk+ = Local softness nucleophilic attack = S fk+
23  wk- = Local philicity index of electrophilic attack = w fk-
24  wk+ = Local philicity index of nucleophilic attack = w fk+

26  hardnessk+ = Local hardness = mu+ fk+ - mu- fk- + (mu+- mu-)*(fk+-fk-)
